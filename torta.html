This torta recipe may take some time depending on the variations of it, as it is a variation of a sandwhich

The Mexican torta come in different verieties as all sandwhiches are. In this section, I'm going to list the ingredient of my favorite type of torta, the Carne Asada Torta

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of roast meat
  • 2 Tellera rolls as buns
  • Mayonnaise as a spread
  • 1/2 cup of refried beans
  • Avocado (either sliced or as spread)
  • 1 Cup of strip letturce
  • 1 Sliced tomato
  • 1 sliced jalapenos

    • Steps

  • Season the meat how you like it or purchase it already seasoned and ready to cook.
  • Open the rolls and spread some mayo over the surface inside
  • Put the rolls over a griddle or grill and heat until lightly toasted.
  • At the same time, grill the meat until it's cooked, but still tender.
  • Remove the buns from the heat and spread some refried beans on them.
  • Immediately add pieces of meat, then slices of avocado, some lettuce and then tomatoes.
  • Add a few pieces of jalape√±o to give it a spicy touch. Serve immediately.
